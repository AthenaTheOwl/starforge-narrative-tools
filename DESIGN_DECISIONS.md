# Starforge Canticles RPG - Design Decisions

## 1. Party Composition: 4 Active + 3 Reserve

- **7 Total Characters:** Avyanna, Elia, Elisira, Vesper, Jalen, Nyx, Rho
- Player controls 4 characters in combat
- 3 in reserve (swap between fights, not mid-combat)
- Act 1 finale: Party splits into Team A (4 chars) + Team B (3 chars)
- All 7 characters available by Chapter 9
- Reserve gains 50% XP (stay relevant)

**Rationale:** Easier to balance, forces strategic choice, matches tactical RPG conventions

## 2. Leveling System: Start Level 1, End Level 5

- **Level 1:** Tutorial (start)
- **Level 2:** 300 XP (after 2-3 early fights, Chapter 2)
- **Level 3:** 600 XP (mid-act, Chapter 5)
- **Level 4:** 900 XP (late-act, Chapter 8)
- **Level 5:** 1200 XP (Act 1 finale, Chapter 10)

**XP Sources:**
- Combat: 50 XP per enemy
- Story milestones: 100-200 XP
- Quest completion: 150 XP

**Rationale:** Steady progression, 5 levels = meaningful growth without being grindy

## 3. Stat Growth: Auto + Manual

**Automatic per level:**
- +5 Max HP
- +2 Max Heat capacity

**Manual allocation (1 point):**
- Attack: +5% ability damage
- Defense: +3 armor, +2 shield, +1 ward
- Lattice: +5% resonance damage, +2% crit chance

**Rationale:** Auto-growth ensures everyone progresses, manual choice allows build customization

## 4. Combat Pacing: 12 Total Encounters

- **Tutorial (Chapter 1):** 1 fight (2 enemies, easy)
- **Early (Chapters 2-3):** 3 fights (introduce mechanics)
- **Mid (Chapters 4-7):** 5 fights (varied enemy types)
- **Late (Chapters 8-9):** 2 fights (challenging)
- **Bosses:** 2 fights (mid-boss Chapter 5, finale Chapter 10)

**Difficulty curve:** Easy → Medium → Hard → Very Hard

**Rationale:** ~1 fight per hour of story, keeps combat fresh without overshadowing narrative

## 5. Ability System: 10 Abilities, 6 Slotted

- Each character starts with 4 abilities
- Unlock 2 new abilities per level (choose 1)
- Total pool: 10 abilities by Level 5
- Equipped: 6 slots (Core1, Core2, Utility1, Utility2, Reaction, Keystone)
- Can respec abilities between fights

**Rationale:** Build variety, meaningful choices, respec allows experimentation
