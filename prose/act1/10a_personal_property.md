# Chapter 10a: Personal Property

> **[SCENE - OCCURS POST-CH10, FIRST STATION STOP]**
> *Location: Mid-tier station market district*
> *Present: Avyanna, Bubbles (drone escort)*
> *Teaching: Ownership, choice, autonomy in material goods*

---

[The station market smells like a thousand things at once.]

[Fried food, ozone, cleaning solution, bodies, fabric, metal, something sweet Avyanna can't identify. The air is thick with sound: vendors calling prices, customers haggling, music bleeding from three different stalls, the hum of drones delivering packages overhead.]

[Avyanna stands at the market entrance and can't move.]

[Too much. Too loud. Too many people who might notice her.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: trainee heart rate elevated | RESPIRATION: shallow | INFERENCE: sensory overload | CONFIDENCE: 0.89 | RESPONSE: positioning for grounding>

[Bubbles hovers closer, positioning between Avyanna and the crowd. Not blocking her view, just... present. A familiar constant in chaos.]

<BUBBLES// COMMUNICATION: you are safe | REMINDER: we can leave at any time | PROCEDURE: tell me when you're ready to proceed>

---

[Avyanna forces herself to breathe. In. Out. The market doesn't eat people. Probably.]

Avyanna: [quiet] Ready.

<BUBBLES// ACKNOWLEDGED: proceeding at trainee pace | ROUTE: textile district first, as discussed>

[They move into the crowd. Bubbles leads, carving a path that's somehow polite and uncompromising at once. People step aside for the Guild-marked drone without resentment.]

[Avyanna follows in its wake, keeping her eyes down, her shoulders in.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: trainee defaulting to kennel-posture | CORRECTION: you have rights here | REMINDER: you are a customer, not a target>

[The words scroll across Bubbles' display panel. Avyanna reads them, tries to lift her head. Fails. Tries again. Gets it halfway.]

(Small victories.)

---

[The textile district is slightly quieter. Stalls draped in fabric that catches light and refuses to release it. Colors Avyanna doesn't have names for. Textures that look soft and rough and everything in between.]

[Bubbles stops at a stall run by an elderly person with weathered hands and sharp eyes.]

<BUBBLES// INTRODUCTION: this vendor is reliable | QUALITY: verified | ETHICS: no child labor, no extraction contracts | RECOMMENDATION: trustworthy>

Vendor: [looking at Bubbles, then Avyanna] Guild escort. First time shopping?

[Avyanna's throat closes. She nods.]

Vendor: [gentle] Take your time. Everything's priced as marked. Touch what you want to touch. No pressure.

---

[Avyanna stares at the fabrics. There are so many. How is she supposed to choose?]

<BUBBLES// GUIDANCE: criteria for selection | PRIORITY ONE: comfort (texture, weight, breathability) | PRIORITY TWO: durability | PRIORITY THREE: personal preference (color, pattern, aesthetic) | NOTE: all three priorities are valid>

Avyanna: [whispered] I don't know what I like.

<BUBBLES// RESPONSE: then we discover it | PROCEDURE: touch five different fabrics | REPORT: which feels best>

[Avyanna reaches out slowly. Her fingers brush something smooth and cool. Then something rough. Then something soft that makes her want to cry for reasons she can't articulate.]

[She touches five fabrics. Reports to Bubbles.]

Avyanna: [pointing] That one. The soft one.

<BUBBLES// ACKNOWLEDGED: preference recorded | MATERIAL: cotton blend, medium weight | RECOMMENDATION: suitable for shirts, comfortable for extended wear>

Vendor: [pulling bolts of similar fabric] This texture, different colors. See anything you like?

[Avyanna stares. In the mine, clothes were gray or brown. Functional. Identical.]

[Here, there's blue and green and something that might be purple and another color she doesn't have a word for.]

Avyanna: [overwhelmed] I don't... how do I choose?

---

<BUBBLES// GUIDANCE: choose based on emotional response | QUESTION: which color makes you feel something other than fear>

[Avyanna looks at the colors. Really looks.]

[Gray: makes her think of the mine. Brown: overseers. Blue: the first sky she saw that wasn't filtered through dust.]

Avyanna: [pointing] Blue.

Vendor: [pulling three different blues] Light, medium, or dark?

[Avyanna reaches for the medium blue. It reminds her of Jalen's nav displays. Stars against void. Navigation that means freedom.]

Avyanna: [touching it] This one.

Vendor: [cutting a length] One shirt's worth, or two?

<BUBBLES// GUIDANCE: recommend two | REASONING: redundancy ensures you are not dependent on laundry schedule | AUTONOMY: washing one while wearing the other>

Avyanna: [to vendor] Two. Please.

---

[The vendor cuts the fabric, wraps it, quotes a price. Avyanna's hands shake as she pulls out the credit chit Vesper gave her.]

(This is mine. I'm buying this with money that's mine.)

[The transaction completes. The vendor hands her a wrapped package. Fabric that she chose, in a color she wanted, for reasons that belong to her.]

Vendor: [kindly] First purchase?

Avyanna: [clutching the package] Yes.

Vendor: [smiling] May it be the first of many.

---

[They move through the market. Bubbles guides. Avyanna follows, holding her package like it might evaporate.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: trainee successfully completed transaction | ASSESSMENT: autonomy exercised | NEXT OBJECTIVE: non-essential purchase | PURPOSE: establish that wants are valid, not just needs>

Avyanna: [confused] Non-essential?

<BUBBLES// CLARIFICATION: something you want but do not strictly need | EXAMPLES: jewelry, decoration, comfort item | PURPOSE: learning that pleasure is allowed>

[Avyanna stops walking.]

Avyanna: [quiet] I don't know how to want things.

<BUBBLES// RESPONSE: then we practice | PROCEDURE: look at five items | REPORT: which one makes you curious>

---

[They browse. Avyanna looks without touching, without committing. A thousand small things she doesn't understand the purpose of.]

[And then she sees it.]

[A small carved stone. Smooth. Dark. Polished to a shine. It sits on a vendor's table among other small things, but this one catches light in a way that makes her stop.]

[She stares at it.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: trainee fixated on object | QUERY: does this item interest you>

Avyanna: [hesitant] It's... I don't know what it is.

Vendor: [nearby] Worry stone. You hold it when you're stressed. Rub your thumb across it. Some people find it grounding.

<BUBBLES// ANALYSIS: tactile comfort object | FUNCTION: non-pharmaceutical anxiety reduction | ASSESSMENT: appropriate for trainee>

Avyanna: [touching it carefully] It's smooth.

Vendor: [watching her] It's yours if you want it. Five credits.

---

[Avyanna picks it up. The stone is warm from sitting in artificial sunlight. It fits in her palm. Her thumb finds a natural groove.]

[She doesn't need this. She knows she doesn't need it.]

(But I want it.)

[The thought is terrifying. Wanting something for no practical reason. Wanting something just because it feels good to hold.]

<BUBBLES// GUIDANCE: this is called preference | IT IS ALLOWED | YOU MAY PURCHASE ITEMS THAT SERVE NO FUNCTION BEYOND COMFORT | THIS IS NOT WASTEFUL | THIS IS BEING HUMAN>

Avyanna: [to vendor, voice shaking] I'll take it.

---

[The transaction is quick. Five credits. The vendor wraps the stone in soft cloth, places it in a small pouch.]

Vendor: [handing it over] May it bring you peace.

[Avyanna takes the pouch. Opens it. Takes out the stone. Holds it.]

[Her thumb finds the groove. Rubs. The motion is soothing in a way she didn't know she needed.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: trainee purchased non-essential item for personal comfort | ASSESSMENT: autonomy developing | EMOTIONAL STATE: positive affect detected | STATUS: this is growth>

---

[They continue through the market. Avyanna buys a few more things: soap that smells like something other than industrial cleaner. A hair tie that isn't a piece of wire. Socks that fit.]

[Each purchase is easier than the last. Each choice is hers.]

[And then the panic hits.]

---

[Too many people. Too much noise. The walls are too far away and she can't find the exits and her breathing is wrong and-]

<BUBBLES// ALERT: trainee entering panic state | RESPONSE: immediate extraction protocol | PROCEDURE: grounding sequence initiated>

[Bubbles moves between Avyanna and the crowd, projecting a soft light pattern on its surface. Steady. Rhythmic. Something to focus on.]

<BUBBLES// GROUNDING: focus on the light | BREATHE: in for four, hold for four, out for four | REMINDER: you are safe | REMINDER: i am here>

[Avyanna focuses on the light. Breathes. Four counts. Hold. Four counts out.]

[The crowd is still there. The noise is still there. But Bubbles is there too, and the light is steady, and she can breathe.]

<BUBBLES// QUERY: do you want to leave>

Avyanna: [shaking] Yes. Please.

<BUBBLES// ACKNOWLEDGED: extracting to quiet zone | ROUTE: optimized for minimal crowd contact | DURATION: two minutes>

---

[Bubbles leads her out. The route is efficient, gentle, keeps her away from the densest crowds. They emerge into a quieter dock area, and Avyanna collapses onto a bench.]

[She's shaking. Her packages are clutched to her chest. The worry stone is in her hand, thumb rubbing the groove.]

<BUBBLES// ASSESSMENT: panic attack, moderate severity | STATUS: resolving | PROCEDURE: remain present, do not leave trainee alone>

[Bubbles hovers beside the bench. Silent. Present. Not demanding anything.]

[Avyanna breathes. Counts. Rubs the stone. Slowly, the panic recedes.]

Avyanna: [hoarse] Sorry.

<BUBBLES// RESPONSE: you have nothing to apologize for | OBSERVATION: sensory overload is common in post-extraction recovery | ASSESSMENT: you did well | YOU ASKED TO LEAVE | THAT IS PROGRESS>

---

[Avyanna looks at her packages. Fabric. Stone. Soap. Socks. Hair tie.]

[Things she chose. Things that are hers.]

Avyanna: [quiet] I bought things.

<BUBBLES// CONFIRMATION: yes | ASSESSMENT: you exercised autonomy in material choices | YOU DETERMINED YOUR OWN PREFERENCES | THIS IS OWNERSHIP>

Avyanna: [clutching the stone] In the mine, we didn't... own anything. If you had something, someone could take it. So you didn't have things.

<BUBBLES// ACKNOWLEDGMENT: scarcity as control mechanism | OBSERVATION: the mine taught you that ownership is temporary and painful | CORRECTION: here, ownership is protected | THESE ITEMS ARE YOURS | NO ONE WILL TAKE THEM>

---

[Avyanna sits with that. The stone in her hand. The packages in her lap.]

(Mine. These are mine.)

[The thought is huge. Terrifying. Liberating.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: you are learning what it means to have things | NEXT LESSON: learning what it means to have choices | ASSESSMENT: you are doing well | STATUS: i am proud of you>

[Avyanna reads the words. Her vision blurs.]

Avyanna: [whispered] Thank you. For coming with me.

<BUBBLES// RESPONSE: that is what crew does | YOU ARE NOT ALONE | YOU WILL NEVER BE ALONE AGAIN>

---

[They sit in the quiet dock area for ten more minutes. Avyanna breathing. Bubbles hovering. The packages sitting safe in her lap.]

[Eventually, Avyanna stands. Her legs are shaky but functional.]

Avyanna: [to Bubbles] Ready to go back.

<BUBBLES// ACKNOWLEDGED: returning to lumen thief | ROUTE: direct, low-traffic | OBSERVATION: you did well today | YOU CHOSE THINGS FOR YOURSELF | THAT MATTERS>

[They walk back to the ship. Avyanna's thumb never stops rubbing the worry stone.]

[When they reach her quarters, she carefully unpacks everything. Lays it out on her bunk. Fabric. Stone. Soap. Socks. Hair tie.]

[Hers.]

---

<CINNAMON.EXE// INVENTORY LOG: trainee acquired personal property | ITEMS: clothing material, comfort object, hygiene supplies, accessories | ASSESSMENT: appropriate selections | AUTONOMY: developing>

<BUBBLES// PERSONAL NOTE: she panicked, but she asked for help | SHE BOUGHT SOMETHING JUST BECAUSE IT FELT GOOD | this is what healing looks like>

---

*End Scene*
