# Chapter 17e: Halcyon's Hands

> **[INTERSTITIAL - OCCURS POST-CH17]**
> *Trigger: Nyx says "witness" like it's a verb*
> *Purpose: show Sutured practice as discipline, not myth*

---

[Nyx's lessons are quiet in the way a blade is quiet.]

[Avyanna finds them in a corner of the ship that smells faintly of herbs and ozone, surrounded by small ward-stones laid out like a careful argument.]

Nyx: Sit.

[Avyanna sits.]

Nyx: Breathe.

[Avyanna breathes.]

Nyx: Now witness.

Avyanna: [frowning] Witness what.

Nyx: Your body.

Nyx: Your fear.

Nyx: The story you keep telling yourself about what fear means.

[Avyanna's jaw tightens.]

Avyanna: Fear means I'm about to get punished.

Nyx: [calm] That is one story.

Nyx: Not the only one.

[Nyx's fingers trace the edge of a ward-stone.]

[Avyanna watches their hands.]

[They move like someone taught them how to touch danger without flinching.]

---

## Intercut - Halcyon Vess (Ward-Keeping)

[The room is bright, but not warm.]

[Stone walls. Clean floors. A training space that pretends it's neutral.]

[Nyx is younger here-still a student, still sharp-edged in the way people are when they haven't learned what softness costs.]

[Across from them, Halcyon stands with a ward-bundle in their hands.]

[Halcyon's hair is tied back. Their sleeves are rolled to the elbow. Their eyes are steady.]

Halcyon: Again.

[Nyx redraws the ward circle with chalk.]

[The line wobbles.]

Halcyon: Stop.

[Nyx freezes.]

Halcyon: Witness the wobble.

Nyx: I saw it.

Halcyon: You noticed it.

Halcyon: That's not the same.

[Nyx's jaw clenches.]

Nyx: It's chalk. It doesn't matter.

Halcyon: It matters because you decided it doesn't.

Halcyon: That decision is how people die in corridors.

[Nyx swallows.]

Halcyon: You don't get to be vague.

Halcyon: Witness is precision.

---

[Halcyon kneels beside the chalk line without crossing it.]

[They don't fix it for Nyx.]

[They point.]

Halcyon: Where did you exhale.

Nyx: [confused] Here.

Halcyon: Your breath moved your hand.

Nyx: That's ridiculous.

Halcyon: It's physics.

Halcyon: Physics doesn't care if you respect it.

[Nyx's mouth tightens.]

Nyx: What do you want me to do.

Halcyon: Say it out loud.

Halcyon: "My breath moved my hand."

[Nyx glares.]

Halcyon: No penalty for honesty.

[Nyx's throat works.]

Nyx: My breath moved my hand.

[Halcyon nods.]

Halcyon: Good.

Halcyon: Now you can correct it.

---

[Nyx redraws the circle.]

[This time, they breathe differently.]

[The line holds.]

[The ward-stone hums faintly.]

Halcyon: Witness.

Nyx: The line is stable.

Halcyon: The line is stable because you were willing to see what caused it to fail.

Halcyon: That is the whole job.

[Nyx looks up.]

Nyx: And if someone is bleeding and the ward fails anyway.

Halcyon: Then you witness that too.

Halcyon: And you don't lie about what you could have done.

Halcyon: And you don't pretend your shame is the same thing as responsibility.

[Nyx's eyes sting.]

Nyx: That sounds cruel.

Halcyon: It's mercy.

Halcyon: To the truth.

---

## Return - Ship Corner

[Nyx's fingers stop moving.]

Nyx: Witness is not prayer.

Nyx: It's method.

[Avyanna swallows.]

Avyanna: Method for what.

Nyx: For not becoming a liar when you're scared.

[A beat.]

Nyx: For not turning other people into acceptable losses to protect your own story.

[Avyanna's chest tightens.]

Avyanna: Okay.

Nyx: Good.

Nyx: Again.

[Nyx pushes a ward-stone toward her.]

[Avyanna picks it up like it might bite.]

[It doesn't.]

[It just sits in her hand, heavy, waiting to be witnessed honestly.]

---
*End Chapter 17e*
