# Chapter 13g: Coffee, Before It Was a Weapon

> **[INTERSTITIAL - OCCURS POST-CH13b]**
> *Trigger: Rho's coffee is care, not just habit*
> *Purpose: show Rho's "governance" started as love*

---

[Rho makes coffee like he's sealing a hull breach.]

[Measured. Repeated. Mildly furious at physics.]

[Avyanna watches from the galley doorway with the same caution she uses around armed strangers.]

[Not because Rho is dangerous.]

[Because tenderness is.]

Rho: [without looking up] You going to stare all day or drink something.

Avyanna: I'm not staring.

Rho: You're staring.

[He sets a mug on the counter and slides it toward her.]

[No ceremony. No "you earned it." No invoice tone.]

Avyanna: [quiet] Why do you care so much.

Rho: About coffee.

Avyanna: About... making it right.

[Rho's hand pauses mid-stir.]

[A small stillness, like a door shutting gently.]

Rho: Habit.

[The word is true and not true.]

---

## Intercut - *Grasp of Dawn* (Rho's First Shift)

[The galley is smaller.]

[The ship is smaller too-old metal, cheap insulation, a floor that rattles when the engines push.]

[Rho is younger and not yet tired in the particular ways that never go away.]

[He is holding a mug with both hands like it's a promise.]

[Across from him, Kenna sits cross-legged on the bench seat, hair tied back with a strip of wire because she is an engineer and everything becomes a tool.]

Kenna: That is not coffee.

Rho: [defensive] It's hot.

Kenna: That's not the definition.

[Kenna leans over his shoulder and adjusts the grinder setting with gentle violence.]

Rho: Don't.

Kenna: I'm helping.

Rho: You always say that like it's a legal defense.

Kenna: [mouth twitching] It is.

---

[Kenna watches the brew drip, eyes half-lidded like she's listening to the ship's bones.]

Kenna: You're scared.

Rho: I'm fine.

Kenna: That's also not the definition.

[Rho exhales.]

Rho: New job.

Kenna: I read the contract.

Rho: I read it too.

Kenna: [flat] Then you're scared on purpose.

[Rho looks away.]

Kenna: Hey.

[Her hand hovers near his wrist.]

[She doesn't grab.]

[She waits for consent like it's normal.]

Rho: Yes.

[Kenna touches him, steady pressure, not a claim.]

Kenna: If it goes bad, we leave.

Rho: The paperwork-

Kenna: [sharp] The paperwork doesn't get to kill us.

[Rho's throat tightens.]

Rho: I don't want to lose the crew.

Kenna: Then feed them.

Rho: [confused] What.

Kenna: You do this.

[She points at the coffee.]

Kenna: You make something small and normal and yours.

Kenna: You make the ship feel like a place people come back to.

Kenna: That's governance.

[Rho makes a face.]

Rho: You sound like Rowan.

Kenna: I sound like someone who wants you alive.

---

[Kenna tastes the coffee, then nods once-approval that lands like a medal.]

Kenna: Better.

Rho: You fixed it.

Kenna: I corrected your assumptions.

Rho: That's the same thing.

Kenna: It is not.

Kenna: But I love that you tried.

[Rho laughs, startled by how easy it is.]

[Kenna smiles like she did that on purpose.]

---

## Return - Galley (Now)

[Rho's hand resumes stirring like the pause never happened.]

[Avyanna watches his face for grief.]

[He gives her none.]

Rho: Drink it before it dies.

Avyanna: Coffee dies.

Rho: Everything dies.

[A beat.]

Rho: This is just faster.

[Avyanna lifts the mug.]

[It smells like bitter warmth and competence.]

Avyanna: [soft] It's good.

[Rho grunts like he doesn't know what to do with praise.]

Rho: That's because I'm excellent.

[He is not smiling.]

[He is, somehow, making the galley feel like a place people come back to.]

---
*End Chapter 13g*
