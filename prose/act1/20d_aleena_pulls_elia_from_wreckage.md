# Chapter 20d: Aleena's Hand

> **[INTERSTITIAL - OCCURS POST-CH20]**
> *Trigger: Avyanna watches Elia move through the ship like it belongs to her*
> *Purpose: Aleena as origin of "you coming?" as invitation, not leash*

---

[After "The Receipt," the ship feels too bright.]

[Not because the lights changed.]

[Because Avyanna's brain is doing what it always does when something impossible happens:]

[Replaying it until it becomes survivable.]

[She walks the corridor to bleed off the energy.]

[She finds Elia in the maintenance bay, stripped down to a tank and cargo pants, wiping her blades with a cloth that looks too ordinary to be allowed near relic steel.]

[Elia glances up and clocks Avyanna's posture in half a second.]

Elia: You're vibrating.

Avyanna: I'm not.

Elia: You are.

[Elia's mouth quirks.]

Elia: Want to break something.

Avyanna: No.

Elia: [delighted] Lies.

[Avyanna surprises herself by almost smiling.]

---

[Elia sets the cloth down.]

Elia: What's the spiral.

Avyanna: Vesper said-

[Avyanna stops.]

[She doesn't know how to say "family" without choking.]

Elia: [softer, still blunt] Yeah.

Elia: That'll do it.

[Avyanna's gaze flicks to Elia's hands.]

[Scarred. Precise. Calm with weapons.]

Avyanna: How did you get like this.

[Elia blinks.]

Elia: Like what.

Avyanna: Like you're... not scared.

[Elia laughs once, sharp.]

Elia: I'm terrified.

Elia: I just don't let it drive.

[A beat.]

Elia: Aleena taught me that.

[The name lands with weight. Avyanna has heard it in scraps, in the way Elisira and Elia say it like a rule.]

Avyanna: Who was she.

[Elia's eyes go distant.]

---

## Intercut - Wreckage (Aleena Finds a Child)

[Smoke.]

[Hot metal.]

[The kind of silence that comes after alarms die and before the survivors start screaming.]

[Elia is a small bundle in the wreckage.]

[Too young to understand what happened, only old enough to understand that everything hurts and no one is answering.]

[A shadow falls over her.]

[Not a supervisor shadow.]

[A different kind: broad-shouldered, steady, moving through debris like it's familiar.]

[Aleena Lagrange kneels.]

[Her face is smeared with soot. There is blood on her sleeve that isn't hers. Her eyes are bright with anger that doesn't spill onto the child.]

[Aleena doesn't reach in and grab.]

[She clears a piece of twisted plating first.]

[Makes space.]

[Then she holds out her hand.]

Aleena: Hey.

[Elia makes a sound that could be a sob, could be a laugh.]

Aleena: You hurt.

Aleena: That's real.

Aleena: But you're alive.

[Elia stares at the offered hand like it's a myth.]

Aleena: You can stay.

Aleena: Or you can come with me.

[A pause.]

Aleena: No penalty.

[Elia doesn't know what "penalty" means.]

[She knows what "come with me" means.]

[It means there is a direction.]

[A floor.]

[Elia reaches out with shaking fingers.]

[Aleena closes her hand around Elia's wrist, not tight, not taking, just... confirming contact.]

Aleena: You coming?

[Elia doesn't have words.]

[Elia nods.]

---

## Return - Maintenance Bay

[Elia's gaze snaps back to Avyanna like she realized she left the room for too long.]

Elia: She found me in wreckage.

Elia: Asked.

Elia: Didn't take.

[Avyanna's throat tightens.]

Avyanna: She said "you coming?"

Elia: [a small nod] Yeah.

Elia: That's the whole thing.

Elia: It's not a command.

Elia: It's a door.

[Elia picks up the cloth again, wipes a blade edge with ritual care.]

Elia: Sometimes you hold the door.

Elia: Sometimes you walk through it.

Elia: Sometimes you kick it down.

[She looks up, eyes bright with something that might be grief and might be joy.]

Elia: But it's always your feet.

[Avyanna swallows.]

Avyanna: Okay.

Elia: [crooked] Good.

Elia: Now go do whatever your vibrating problem is.

Avyanna: [deadpan] That was emotional.

Elia: [immediately defensive] It was practical.

[Avyanna walks out of the bay with her chest tight and her spine a fraction straighter.]

[A door, offered.]

[A phrase that means: choose.]

---
*End Chapter 20d*
