# Chapter 13e: Water Is Not a Test

> **[SCENE - OCCURS POST-CH13d, EARLY REST PRACTICE]**
> *Location: Crew quarters washroom / laundry nook*
> *Present: Avyanna, Bubbles, Cinnamon.exe*
> *Teaching: comfort is not a quota; care is not a reward*

---

[The washroom is too clean.]

[Not sterile-lived clean. The kind of clean that means someone uses this space and then puts it back the way they want to find it.]

[Avyanna stands in the doorway with her hands in fists, staring at the shower control panel.]

[A dial with temperature ranges. A toggle for pressure. A little display that says WATER AVAILABLE.]

(Available for who. For how long.)

[Bubbles hovers behind her, not pushing her in.]

Bubbles: You have not bathed.

Avyanna: [too fast] I have.

[Bubbles waits.]

[Avyanna hears herself and corrects the lie without being asked.]

Avyanna: I rinsed.

Avyanna: Once.

Bubbles: That is not bathing.

Avyanna: It was enough.

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: deflection language | INFERENCE: scarcity conditioning | CONFIDENCE: 0.89>

[Avyanna stares at the dial.]

[She can feel the mine on her skin like a second uniform: dust that never leaves, sweat that dries wrong, the memory of water that came with eyes on you.]

(If you take too long, someone yells. If you take too much, someone takes it back.)

[Cinnamon's terminal in the corner wakes, as if it has been listening and decides it is time to be useful.]

<CINNAMON.EXE// STATUS: water rationing not active | NOTE: no metering policy for hygiene | POLICY: "take what you need">

Avyanna: [hoarse] Nothing is unmetered.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: correct in hostile systems | NOTE: this ship is not hostile to hygiene>

Avyanna: That doesn't mean it's safe.

[Bubbles rotates toward her, fans quieting.]

Bubbles: You are safe enough to be clean.

[The sentence lands like a hand offered.]

[Avyanna doesn't take it. Not yet.]

Avyanna: If I use too much, does it... affect the ship.

[Cinnamon answers without pity, which is the only way Avyanna can hear it.]

<CINNAMON.EXE// ANSWER: negligible impact | CONTEXT: hygiene is operational requirement | FOLLOW-UP: infections cost more water>

Avyanna: [a small laugh that isn't humor] Everything is operational.

<CINNAMON.EXE// AFFIRMATION: yes>

Bubbles: We like you operational.

Avyanna: [quiet] I'm not a machine.

[Bubbles' fans change pitch-annoyed in defense of her.]

Bubbles: Correct.

Bubbles: You are a person.

[Avyanna swallows.]

[She reaches for the dial and stops.]

[Her hand hovers over warmth like it's a trap door.]

(Warm water means vulnerability. Warm water means someone can hurt you because you're soft.)

Avyanna: [barely] How long.

Bubbles: How long what.

Avyanna: How long until I'm... done.

[Silence. Listening, not judging.]

<CINNAMON.EXE// OBSERVATION: trainee framing hygiene as timed task | INFERENCE: prior punishment context | CONFIDENCE: 0.91>

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: there is no "until" | SUGGESTION: bathe until body reports "better">

Avyanna: My body lies.

[Bubbles drifts closer, just enough that Avyanna can hear the tiny corrective hum.]

Bubbles: Your body is a witness.

Bubbles: It is scared. It is still a witness.

[Avyanna's throat tightens.]

Avyanna: That's not fair.

Bubbles: No.

Bubbles: The mine was not fair.

[Avyanna turns the dial one notch toward warm.]

[Water hisses through the pipes like the ship clearing its throat.]

[She flinches at the sound.]

[Bubbles does not comment.]

[The first spray hits her palm-hot enough to make her jerk back.]

Avyanna: Ow.

<CINNAMON.EXE// OBSERVATION: temperature overshoot | ACTION: reduce to safe threshold>

[The water shifts to a steady warmth, like someone smoothing a blanket.]

Avyanna: [suspicious] You adjusted it.

<CINNAMON.EXE// AFFIRMATION: yes | NOTE: you are allowed to be comfortable>

[Avyanna stares at the display again.]

[WATER AVAILABLE.]

[It doesn't tick down.]

(It doesn't tick down.)

[Her shoulders drop by a millimeter.]

[She steps in.]

[The water hits her hair, her scalp, her neck. Heat spreads under skin that has been cold for too long.]

[For a second, the mine tries to rise-showers as a line, as a punishment, as a place where eyes measured you for weakness.]

[Avyanna's lungs seize.]

[She turns her face into the spray and forces breath through the moment.]

[In. Out.]

[In.]

[Bubbles speaks from outside the curtain, not entering without permission.]

Bubbles: Do you want company.

Avyanna: [immediate] No.

[Then-because she is learning honesty is a floor, not a threat-]

Avyanna: Thank you.

Bubbles: You are welcome.

[Cinnamon's text appears on the terminal, simple.]

<CINNAMON.EXE// REMINDER: you may take two towels>

Avyanna: [startled] Two.

<CINNAMON.EXE// CLARIFICATION: yes | NOTE: drying quickly reduces chill response>

[She laughs again, quieter.]

Avyanna: Who decided that.

<CINNAMON.EXE// ANSWER: me>

Avyanna: [muttering] Of course.

[She washes. Slowly.]

[Soap that smells like something alive-not mine disinfectant, not chemical fear.]

[When she finally turns the water off, she stands still for a moment, waiting for the other shoe.]

[No alarm.]

[No banging on the door.]

[No shouted quota.]

[Just the ship humming like it has always been this kind.]

[Avyanna steps out wrapped in a towel that is too soft to trust.]

[Bubbles offers another without making it a question that costs anything.]

[Avyanna takes it.]

[Her hands tremble once.]

(I'm clean. And nothing bad happened.)

[She doesn't know what to do with that.]

[So she does the only thing she knows how to do with new evidence.]

[She files it.]

---

[The laundry nook is adjacent, like the ship assumes hygiene includes clothing and isn't making you ask permission twice.]

[A row of baskets. A machine that looks too expensive to waste on ordinary fabric. Shelves of detergent in small bottles, each labeled with a scent name that feels like a luxury someone bothered to catalog.]

[Avyanna stands in front of them as if choosing wrong will be punished.]

(In the mine, you got whatever chemical soap they issued. It stripped skin. It didn't count as cruelty because it was "standard.")

[Bubbles hovers at the doorway again, refusing to enter without being invited.]

Bubbles: Laundry is next.

Avyanna: [stiff] I can do it.

Bubbles: Yes.

Bubbles: Do you want help.

[Avyanna's throat tightens at the question. Help always meant debt.]

Avyanna: No.

[Then-]

Avyanna: Maybe... explain the machine.

[Bubbles' fans shift: approval without fanfare.]

Bubbles: Yes.

---

[The machine has a simple interface.]

[*Load.*]

[*Select cycle.*]

[*Select temperature.*]

[*Start.*]

[No cost display. No countdown. No warning about "overuse."]

Avyanna: [quiet] It doesn't track.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: it tracks for maintenance only | NOTE: not for punishment>

[Avyanna pulls her clothes from the hamper.]

[Crew-issue fabric, already softening from regular washing.]

[She hesitates at the pile, suddenly aware of how much she owns.]

(Mine brain: hoard. Keep. Don't waste. Don't take what you can't defend.)

[She starts sorting like she's trying to prove she deserves it.]

[Bubbles doesn't let her spiral quietly.]

Bubbles: You are permitted to wash all of it.

Avyanna: [defensive] I wasn't-

Bubbles: You were.

[Avyanna freezes.]

Avyanna: [small] If I wash it all, I'll have nothing to wear.

<CINNAMON.EXE// SUGGESTION: use spare linens | NOTE: crew-issue basics exist in supply locker | POLICY: you may take them>

Avyanna: Supply locker.

(The mine would never let you take from supply without "approval." Approval meant debt.)

Bubbles: We can get you two sets of basics.

Avyanna: Two.

Bubbles: Two.

[Bubbles pauses.]

Bubbles: You can have more later.

[Avyanna's chest tightens.]

Avyanna: [rough] I don't know how to...

Bubbles: Want.

[The word is gentle and brutal.]

[Avyanna swallows.]

Avyanna: I don't know how to want without paying.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: practice>

---

[Avyanna stares at the detergent shelf again.]

Avyanna: What do I pick.

<CINNAMON.EXE// QUESTION: what do you prefer>

[Prefer.]

[The word feels like a trick question.]

[Avyanna picks up one bottle, reads the label.]

> *Citrus.*

[She puts it down.]

[Picks up another.]

> *Lavender.*

[Her throat tightens unexpectedly.]

(The mine smelled like metal and sweat and disinfectant. Lavender smells like a place where no one dies for production.)

Avyanna: [quiet] This one.

[No alarm. No correction. No laughter.]

Bubbles: Acknowledged.

<CINNAMON.EXE// NOTE: preference logged | COMMENTARY: good>

["Preference logged" should feel invasive.]

[Instead it feels like someone taking her seriously.]

---

[She loads the machine.]

[Presses start.]

[The drum begins to turn.]

[The sound is ordinary. Domestic. It makes her eyes sting.]

(The ship is doing chores. The ship is treating me like I belong inside chores.)

[Avyanna wipes her face with the edge of her towel before she can be embarrassed by needing one.]

[Bubbles stays outside the doorway, a sentinel for dignity.]

Bubbles: Do you need anything else.

[Avyanna looks at the shelf of folded towels.]

[One is still around her hair. Another around her shoulders. She wants a third, absurdly, because it would mean she's allowed to be wasteful.]

[She takes a breath.]

Avyanna: I... want another towel.

[Silence.]

[Then Cinnamon, brisk as ever:]

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: granted>

[Bubbles adds:]

Bubbles: I will bring it.

[Avyanna's throat tightens. Not with shame. With something worse.]

(They didn't make me justify it.)

[Bubbles returns with a third towel and places it on the counter like it's nothing.]

[Like wanting is nothing.]

[Avyanna doesn't know how to hold that without breaking, so she does what she's learned to do with fragile things.]

[She holds it carefully.]
