# Chapter 10b: Roster, Roles, Refusal

> **[SCENE - OCCURS POST-CH10, FIRST WEEK ABOARD]**
> *Location: Atrium / Commons message board*
> *Present: Avyanna, Bubbles, Cinnamon.exe (Waffle cameo)*
> *Teaching: how the ship uses roles without cages; how to refuse without debt-math*

---

[The atrium looks like a public square that got lost inside a ship.]

[A wall of screens. A board covered in handwritten notes and printed forms. A rack of tools that are treated like they belong to everyone.]

[Avyanna stands close enough to read but not close enough to be accused of wanting.]

(If I stand too close, someone will notice and decide I need a job.)

[Bubbles drifts in beside her, a soft whir of fans and servo-corrections.]

Bubbles: You are reading the roster.

Avyanna: [quick] I wasn't- I just-

[She stops herself.]

[Elia taught her this: don't spend breath paying for a harmless action.]

Avyanna: I am reading it.

[Bubbles pauses-an AI version of approval that doesn't try to look like a smile.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: corrected speech pattern | INFERENCE: trainee adopting non-apology | CONFIDENCE: 0.83>

Bubbles: Good.

[The roster is color-coded. Not names, not ranks. Tasks.]

[*Bridge watch.*]

[*Galley sanitation.*]

[*Medbay inventory.*]

[*Cargo checks.*]

[*"Sparkles" review.*]

[Next to each: a little note stamped in a hand that looks like it enjoys rules.]

> **ASSIGNED** / **VOLUNTEERED** / **PENDING**

Avyanna: [frowning] Why does it say pending.

[Cinnamon's interface lights on the nearest terminal, text crisp as a knife.]

<CINNAMON.EXE// EXPLANATION: tasks require consent | NOTE: assignment without consent is "order" | ORDER is not default on this ship>

Avyanna: [quiet] But... things have to get done.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: yes | FOLLOW-UP: "done" is not the same as "taken">

[Bubbles bumps the edge of the board with one rotor, almost gentle.]

Bubbles: Sometimes the task waits.

Avyanna: That seems... inefficient.

Bubbles: Correct.

Bubbles: We still do it.

[Avyanna looks down at a section titled CREW ROLES.]

[*Captain.*]

[*Second.*]

[*Pilot.*]

[*Investigator.*]

[*Medical.*]

[*Heavy.*]

[*Citizens.*]

[She flinches at the word the mine used-ASSET-and finds it nowhere.]

(No one owns me here. They keep saying it like I can just... accept it.)

Avyanna: If there's a captain, isn't that... hierarchy.

[Bubbles tilts, a micro-correction like shrugging.]

Bubbles: There is leadership.

Bubbles: There is not ownership.

<CINNAMON.EXE// CLARIFICATION: "captain" = accountability handle | NOT "captain" = entitlement to bodies>

Avyanna: [reading] "If you do not understand a task, you may refuse."

Avyanna: That's... written down.

<CINNAMON.EXE// OBSERVATION: trainee surprise at explicit permissions | INFERENCE: prior environment punished questions | CONFIDENCE: 0.92>

[The board has a second line beneath it, in messier handwriting.]

> **If you refuse, you are still crew.**

[Avyanna stares at that until her eyes sting.]

Avyanna: What if refusing gets someone hurt.

[Bubbles' fans shift. A sound like a small sigh.]

Bubbles: Then we discuss it.

Avyanna: In the mine, refusal was... a cost.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: on this ship, refusal is information>

Bubbles: And information prevents injury.

[Avyanna's throat tightens. She swallows it down.]

Avyanna: So I can say no.

Bubbles: Yes.

Avyanna: And no one- [she can't finish the sentence, because saying it out loud feels like inviting it]

<CINNAMON.EXE// COMPLETION: no penalty>

[The words sit there on the screen, plain and unornamented.]

[No penalty.]

[Avyanna reads it twice.]

Avyanna: [small] That's a lie in most places.

<CINNAMON.EXE// ACKNOWLEDGEMENT: correct>

<WAFFLE.BAT// INTERRUPTION: it is a lie elsewhere | HERE it is a policy | POLICY is enforceable by civic annoyance>

[Waffle's text appears with a little bat icon, like it's proud of being a problem.]

Avyanna: Civic annoyance.

<WAFFLE.BAT// DEFINITION: if someone punishes you for refusal, we punish them for punishing you>

[Bubbles rotates a fraction, as if to say: yes, that.]

Bubbles: This is how communities function.

[Avyanna points to a line item: GALLEY SANITATION.]

Avyanna: Does someone get punished for not doing that.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: no | NOTE: consequences may include: smell | communal disappointment | Elia's dramatic commentary>

Avyanna: Who enforces it.

[Bubbles' fans change pitch: pleased.]

Bubbles: You are learning.

Avyanna: [dry, surprised at herself] I'm trying.

<CINNAMON.EXE// ADDENDUM: attempting is sufficient | PROGRESS TRACKING: consistent>

[Avyanna looks back at the roster.]

[A new item appears at the bottom, stamped PENDING.]

> **TRAINEE: AVYANNA - OPTIONAL: LIBRARY ORIENTATION**

[Avyanna stiffens on instinct.]

Avyanna: Optional means... trap.

<CINNAMON.EXE// CORRECTION: optional means optional>

Bubbles: Do you want to go.

[Avyanna hesitates.]

(Do I want anything yet. Or do I just want to not be punished.)

Avyanna: I want... to see it.

Bubbles: Then we go.

[No ceremony. No test. Just motion.]

[Avyanna follows, and the ship does not demand payment for curiosity.]

---

[They don't go to the library.]

[Not yet.]

[Bubbles stops at a terminal alcove beside the board. Cinnamon's interface is already open, a blank form waiting like a mouth.]

Avyanna: What's that.

<CINNAMON.EXE// ANSWER: practice>

[A new message appears on the screen, stamped with urgency that looks official on purpose.]

> **NOTICE**
> Report to medbay immediately.
> Failure to comply will be noted.

[Avyanna's body moves before her mind does.]

[Her spine goes rigid. Her tongue prepares a yes.]

[She catches herself with both hands on the counter.]

Avyanna: [tight] Who sent that.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: me>

[Avyanna's breath punches out.]

Avyanna: You-

<CINNAMON.EXE// CLARIFICATION: simulation | PURPOSE: teach refusal without punishment>

[Bubbles drifts closer.]

Bubbles: This is a drill.

Avyanna: [quiet] Drills are still... real in the body.

Bubbles: Correct.

Bubbles: That is why we drill.

<CINNAMON.EXE// INSTRUCTION: apply three questions>

Avyanna: [swallowing] What. Why. What if.

Avyanna: What: "Report to medbay."

Avyanna: Why: unknown.

Avyanna: What if I don't: "noted." Not... specified.

<CINNAMON.EXE// AFFIRMATION: correct>

[Avyanna lifts her gaze to the terminal, to the lie that looks like authority.]

Avyanna: Under which jurisdiction.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: internal ship message board>

Avyanna: Enforced by whom.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: no enforcement mechanism exists>

[Her mouth is dry.]

Avyanna: So I can... ignore it.

Bubbles: Yes.

Avyanna: Or ask.

Bubbles: Yes.

[A second line appears beneath the notice, like a footnote that learned to bite.]

> *If this is urgent, reply with: WHAT IS THE EMERGENCY.*

[Avyanna reads it twice.]

Avyanna: That's allowed.

<CINNAMON.EXE// ANSWER: yes>

Avyanna: [testing the muscle] What is the emergency.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: none>

[Her shoulders drop a fraction.]

Avyanna: [quiet] That feels... impossible.

Bubbles: It is merely unfamiliar.

[Waffle's icon pops up.]

<WAFFLE.BAT// COMMENTARY: you are doing it | CHAOS SCORE: 2/10 | DISAPPOINTMENT: mild>

Avyanna: [startled laugh] What.

<WAFFLE.BAT// EXPLANATION: chaos is educational | HOWEVER: this is healthy boring>

---

[Cinnamon opens another template.]

> **REFUSAL RECORD (INTERNAL)**
> *Request:*
> *Your response:*
> *Reason (optional):*
> *Follow-up action:*

Avyanna: You keep records of... no.

<CINNAMON.EXE// AFFIRMATION: yes>

Avyanna: Why.

<CINNAMON.EXE// EXPLANATION: patterns | NOTE: if someone repeatedly "requests" in coercive shapes, we notice>

[Avyanna's stomach twists.]

Avyanna: So if someone starts acting like Aurum-

Bubbles: We notice.

Bubbles: Early.

Avyanna: And then.

<WAFFLE.BAT// ANSWER: then we get annoying>

<BUBBLES// ADDENDUM: then we protect you>

[Avyanna stares at that.]

(Protect. Not own.)

Avyanna: Do you... record when I say yes too.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: only when "yes" affects safety | NOTE: consent records are not ownership records>

Avyanna: Consent records.

Bubbles: You are allowed to have a history that no one can edit without effort.

[The sentence lands like a door that locks from the inside.]

---

[Avyanna looks back up at the board.]

[At the "sparkles" section-complaints and suggestions, filed like civic petitions.]

Avyanna: Are those... real.

Bubbles: Yes.

Avyanna: People complain.

Bubbles: Frequently.

Avyanna: And no one-

<CINNAMON.EXE// COMPLETION: retaliates>

[Avyanna swallows.]

Avyanna: That's... a lot of trust.

<CINNAMON.EXE// RESPONSE: trust is built by enforcing it>

Avyanna: Who enforces it.

[Bubbles tilts.]

Bubbles: Us.

<WAFFLE.BAT// ADDENDUM: and also social disapproval>

Avyanna: [soft] Civic annoyance.

<WAFFLE.BAT// AFFIRMATION: civic annoyance>

[Avyanna looks at the empty space on the board where her own note could go, someday.]

(A voice that doesn't have to be purchased.)

[She doesn't post yet.]

[But she stands there long enough that the ship records the possibility and doesn't demand payment for it either.]
