# Chapter 16c: Maintenance Pairing Day

> **[SCENE - OCCURS POST-CH16b, FIRST MONTHLY MAINTENANCE RITUAL]**
> *Location: Ship-wide (pair rotations)*
> *Present: Avyanna + rotating crew; AI citizens coordinate*
> *Teaching: maintenance is care; tools are shared; no one is exempt*

---

[Maintenance day starts with a board posted in the atrium.]

[Not a command. A schedule.]

[Pairs. Tasks. Time blocks.]

[Avyanna reads it twice before she believes her name belongs there.]

> **AVYANNA + RHO** - *Filters / seals / coolant checks (galley-adjacent)*
>
> **AVYANNA + BUBBLES** - *Drone nest inventory (label sanity)*
>
> **AVYANNA + JALEN** - *Bridge conduit inspection (non-invasive)*

(They're trusting me with the ship.)

[Cinnamon's text appears on the terminal beside the board.]

<CINNAMON.EXE// REMINDER: maintenance is preventative, not punitive | NOTE: you cannot "fail" maintenance if you report what you see>

[Avyanna's stomach tightens.]

(In the mine, maintenance meant blame.)

---

[Rho meets her at the galley hatch with a tool bag and the expression of someone approaching a nuisance he loves anyway.]

Rho: You ever change a filter.

Avyanna: [honest] No.

Rho: Good. You won't do it wrong on purpose then.

[He hands her gloves.]

[Not orders. Equipment.]

Rho: Rule one: tell me what you see. Not what you think you're supposed to see.

Avyanna: [quiet] That sounds like Vesper.

Rho: Everybody on this ship is a little bit Vesper.

[He squints.] 

Rho: Except Elia. Elia's a whole different disease.

[Avyanna snorts.]

[They pop a panel. The smell of old air hits Avyanna's face.]

[Rho points at the filter assembly.]

Rho: This is where "out of stock" likes to hide.

Avyanna: [frowning] Out of stock is-

Rho: A claim.

[He taps it with the back of a screwdriver.]

Rho: Not a verdict.

[Avyanna nods, cataloging it as a tool.]

[They work. Slowly.]

[Rho narrates the steps without making her feel stupid.]

[When Avyanna's hand shakes, she pretends it's from torque.]

[Rho doesn't call her out.]

[He just steadies the panel with his own hand so she can use both hands without fear of dropping anything.]

---

[Second rotation: drone nest inventory.]

[Bubbles is already there, surrounded by neat rows of parts that look like jewelry if you don't know they can cut a man in half.]

Bubbles: Hello.

Avyanna: [more steady now] Hi.

[Bubbles projects an inventory list.]

Bubbles: We will count.

Avyanna: [dry] Counting as rebellion.

<BUBBLES// AFFIRMATION: yes>

[They move through bins.]

[Avyanna reads labels out loud.]

Avyanna: "Swarm anchor pins."

Avyanna: "Hinge washers."

Avyanna: "Do not feed Elia."

[She stops.]

Avyanna: Is that a real category.

Bubbles: It is an aspirational category.

[Avyanna laughs.]

[The laugh feels like trespassing.]

[Bubbles doesn't punish it.]

<BUBBLES// OBSERVATION: trainee laughter | INFERENCE: nervous-system downshift | CONFIDENCE: 0.79>

[They find a bin with a label crossed out and replaced in marker.]

> **"EXTRA"** -> **"SPARE"**

Avyanna: [soft] Someone changed it.

Bubbles: I did.

Avyanna: Why.

Bubbles: "Extra" implies waste.

Bubbles: "Spare" implies safety.

[Avyanna swallows.]

(Words matter. Always.)

---

[Third rotation: bridge conduit inspection.]

[Jalen meets her in the corridor outside the bridge with a flashlight and a look that says: don't touch anything, and also: you're allowed to be here.]

Jalen: You're not crawling in.

Avyanna: [relieved] Good.

Jalen: You're observing.

Avyanna: That I can do.

[They open an access panel. Cables. Heat. A faint vibration that feels like the ship's pulse.]

Jalen: See that discoloration.

Avyanna: [leaning in] Yes.

Jalen: What is it.

[Avyanna pauses.]

Avyanna: I don't know.

[Jalen nods, as if "I don't know" is a valid piece of evidence.]

Jalen: Good.

Jalen: Now tell me what you can say without guessing.

Avyanna: The sheath is worn. The color's different near the joint. It looks... heated.

Jalen: Good.

Jalen: That's a claim with observations behind it.

Avyanna: [startled] That's Vesper language.

Jalen: Vesper stole it from physics. Physics stole it from reality.

---

[By the time the last rotation ends, Avyanna's hands are smudged with grease.]

[Her hair smells faintly like metal.]

[She's tired in a way that doesn't feel like punishment.]

(I worked. And no one took anything from me for it.)

[Back in the atrium, the board updates.]

> **MAINTENANCE DAY - COMPLETE**
>
> *Notes: 3 issues logged. 0 blame assigned. 1 coffee spill archived for historical purposes.*

<WAFFLE.BAT// COMMENTARY: coffee spill was heroic>

<CINNAMON.EXE// CORRECTION: coffee spill was avoidable>

<BUBBLES// ADDENDUM: coffee spill was forgiven>

[Avyanna reads that and feels, for a moment, like she lives in a place where forgiveness is a default setting.]

[She doesn't trust it yet.]

[But she's learning how to stand on it anyway.]

---

[The last entry on the board isn't a task.]

> **MAINTENANCE DAY DEBRIEF - OPTIONAL**

[Optional.]

[Avyanna's eyes snag on the word like it's a hook.]

(Optional means: you can choose. Choice means: you can be blamed.)

[She goes anyway.]

[The debrief is in the atrium, where people can hear and therefore things stay honest.]

[Elia lounges on a couch like meetings are allergic to her.]

[Elisira stands with her slate, already having organized everything into categories.]

[Rho smells like coolant and coffee.]

[Nyx is there too, hands stained with something that might be soil or ink.]

[Bubbles hovers. Cinnamon's terminal glows. Waffle's icon blinks like it's trying to behave.]

Elisira: Issues logged.

[She reads without drama.]

Elisira: Galley filter assembly runs hot at joint. Bridge conduit sheath worn. Drone nest bin mislabeled.

Elia: [perking up] What.

Bubbles: The bin labeled "do not feed Elia."

Elia: That's an important label.

Elisira: It is not operationally necessary.

Elia: [affronted] It is culturally necessary.

<CINNAMON.EXE// NOTE: both claims have merit>

[Avyanna's mouth twitches. It's becoming a habit.]

Nyx: Did anyone get injured.

Rho: No.

Nyx: Good. That means maintenance did its job.

Avyanna: [quiet, before she can stop herself] In the mine, maintenance only happened after someone died.

[Silence.]

[Not the kind that punishes. The kind that listens.]

Nyx: Yes.

Nyx: Here, we do it before.

Nyx: That is the difference between extraction and care.

[Avyanna swallows.]

Elisira: Avyanna.

[Avyanna's spine goes rigid.]

Elisira: What did you learn.

[Not "what did you do wrong."]

[What did you learn.]

Avyanna: [working] That reporting a problem isn't... confessing guilt.

Elisira: Correct.

Avyanna: That tools aren't personal property.

[Bubbles' fans shift, pleased.]

Avyanna: That "extra" versus "spare" matters.

Elia: [delighted] Yes. Vocabulary war.

Vesper isn't here.

[And yet Avyanna hears her anyway: words are leverage.]

Rho: Also learned the bridge conduit was cooked.

Jalen: [over comm, dry] It is not cooked. It is lightly toasted.

Elia: Toasted is still edible. Therefore safe.

Jalen: [flat] That is not how ships work.

<WAFFLE.BAT// COMMENTARY: toasted ship is funny concept>

<CINNAMON.EXE// CORRECTION: toasted ship is unacceptable>

---

[Elisira stamps the slate with a final note.]

Elisira: No blame assigned.

[Avyanna's chest tightens.]

Avyanna: That's... written down too.

Elisira: Yes.

Elisira: Because we mean it.

[Avyanna nods once, hard.]

(Say what you mean. Mean what you say.)

[The phrase is becoming a rail inside her.]

[Elia stands, stretches, and claps Avyanna on the shoulder-not hard, not claiming. Just present.]

Elia: You did good, Avy.

Avyanna: [quiet] That's not a metric.

Elia: [grinning] It is my metric.

[Avyanna can't help it.]

[She laughs.]
